


## Blurb for Partiful

TUESDAY BEFORE DEF CON. August 4th 2026! 

Do you want to unwind before unloading?
Drink before drinking?
Hack before hacking?
Win before DEF CON even starts?

Then this is the party for you.

Aask is bringing the Midwest hacker scene and the West Coast hacker scene together for a pre-con IoT firmware dumping bonanza with booze, live music, video game contests, and suspicious little devices that absolutely should not have cloud credentials in them.

Top hackers can win a bag of 100 GOLD COINS.*

Not hacking? Still come. We’ll have 3+ live musicians, including main-stage DEF CON DJs, plus video game contests, drinks, games, and plenty of "drink all the booze, hack all the things" vibes while we wait for the last device to fall and DEF CON gets canceled.

Now featuring CantBeABird!

Location TBD. Approval-only at first while we figure out capacity. Guest list expands as RSVPs come in.

*Not real gold. Obviously.


Bring the weird IoT thing you bought, found, forgot about, or “definitely meant to look at later.” Routers, cameras, smart plugs, mystery boards, cursed cloud trash, bad ideas from Amazon, anything with firmware and regrets.

If there’s no GitHub repo to save you, even better.

Points will be awarded for uniqueness, impact, keys, firmware dumps, weird services, cursed findings, and how many times everyone says “wait, what the fuck?”
